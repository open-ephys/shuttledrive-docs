Parts list#

You will need some parts and tools designed specifically for the shuttleDrive:

Parts#

  • 1 x 3d printed drive body

  • 16 x 3d printed drive shuttles

  • 1m of Polymicro (polyimide-coated quartz) shuttle tubes

  • Polyimide tubes to make guide tube arrays

  • 16 x Custom drive screws (reusable)

  • Electrode interface boards (EIBs, reusable)

  • Gold pins

Tools#

  • Assembly jig

  • Custom screwdriver

(Refer to the full list below for additional tools such as forceps etc.)
